Our Story

4Copola is an alternative metal band unlike anything seen before in Spain, embracing songs from diverse genres without fear of experimentation. In mid-2018, they released their debut album "De lo esotérico a lo carnal" ("From the Esoteric to the Carnal").

This album enabled them to win awards such as the second edition of Tuenti Rock and appear on the lineup of Resurrection Fest.
